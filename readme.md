\# 🎮 Cable



Cable is a fast-paced multiplayer FPS focused on skill, precision, and competitive gameplay.



\## 🚀 About the Game



Cable delivers a pure first-person shooter experience where every match is decided by your aim, movement, and decision-making. No shortcuts — just raw skill.



\## 🔥 Features



\- Fast-paced multiplayer combat  

\- Skill-based gunplay  

\- Smooth and responsive controls  

\- Competitive gameplay  

\- Clean and focused design  



\## ⚔️ Gameplay



Engage in intense matches against other players. Master movement, improve your aim, and outplay your opponents to dominate the battlefield.



\## 🎯 Objective



Outshoot and outmaneuver your enemies. The better your reflexes and strategy, the higher your chances of winning.



\## 🛠️ Tech Stack



\- Game Engine: Unreal Engine 5  

\- Language: Blueprints / C++
